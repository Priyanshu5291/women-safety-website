Personal Safety Tips

  • Stay aware of your surroundings at all times.
  • Avoid walking alone at night in poorly lit areas.
  • Keep your phone charged and easily accessible.
  • Trust your instincts – if something doesn’t feel right, leave immediately.
  • Consider taking a self-defense class to feel more confident in dangerous situations.
  • Always share your plans with a trusted friend or family member when going out.
  • If you feel unsafe, go to a public place or seek help from nearby people.
  • Use safety apps that allow you to share your location with trusted contacts.
